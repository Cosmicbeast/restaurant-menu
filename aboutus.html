About Us

Global Spice Symphony is a vibrant and user-friendly web-based restaurant menu designed to showcase a diverse selection of dishes from around the world. Whether you crave the spices of India, the elegance of Italian pasta, or the bold flavors of Chinese cuisine, our menu brings it all together in one beautiful, responsive interface.

This project was built with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to ensure a seamless experience across devices. From the visual design to the navigation flow, every part has been crafted to make browsing the menu enjoyable and efficient.

Created with passion and creativity, this site reflects the spirit of culinary exploration and the power of clean, modern web design.
