North Indian Cuisine

Appetizers

Paneer Tikka
Cubes of fresh paneer marinated in yogurt and aromatic spices, grilled to perfection in a tandoor.
Chicken Malai Tikka
Tender chicken pieces soaked in a creamy cashew and cheese marinade, delicately spiced and char-grilled.
Aloo Tikki Chaat
Crispy potato patties topped with tangy tamarind chutney, spicy green chutney, yogurt, and sev.
Seekh Kebabs (Mutton)
Minced mutton blended with herbs and spices, molded onto skewers and cooked over charcoal.
Dahi Puri
Hollow, crispy puris filled with potatoes, chickpeas, and sweet yogurt, finished with chutneys.

Main Course

Butter Chicken
Classic grilled chicken simmered in a rich, buttery tomato gravy with a hint of fenugreek.
Dal Makhani
Black lentils and kidney beans slow-cooked overnight with butter and cream for a velvety texture.
Rogan Josh
Aromatic Kashmiri curry with tender mutton cooked in a flavorful gravy of yogurt and spices.
Kadhai Paneer
Paneer and bell peppers stir-fried in a spicy tomato gravy with freshly ground kadhai masala.
Chole Bhature
Fluffy, deep-fried bhature bread served with a spicy and tangy chickpea curry.

South Indian Cuisine

Main Course

Masala Dosa
A thin, crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a savory spiced potato mash.
Mysore Masala Dosa
A spicier dosa with a fiery red chutney spread inside before the potato filling.
Chicken Chettinad
A fiery and aromatic curry made with a blend of roasted spices and succulent chicken.
Andhra Gongura Mutton
A tangy and spicy Andhra specialty featuring tender mutton cooked with sour gongura leaves.

Rice & Accompaniments

Curd Rice / Lemon Rice
Comforting yogurt rice tempered with mustard seeds, or tangy lemon-flavored rice.
Bisibele Bath
A wholesome dish of rice, lentils, and vegetables cooked with tamarind and a special spice blend.
Appam with Stew
Soft, lacy rice pancakes served with a mild vegetable or chicken stew in coconut milk.

Italian Cuisine

Pasta & Mains

Spaghetti (Bolognese / Alfredo)
Classic spaghetti with a rich meat sauce (Bolognese) or a creamy parmesan sauce (Alfredo).
Penne (Arrabiata)
Penne pasta tossed in a spicy tomato sauce made with garlic, tomatoes, and red chili peppers.
Lasagna (Veg / Chicken)
Layers of flat pasta baked with rich béchamel, savory filling, and mozzarella cheese.

Pizza

Margherita
The timeless classic with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.
Four Cheese
A decadent pizza topped with a blend of four cheeses: mozzarella, gorgonzola, parmesan, and provolone.
Pepperoni / Veggie Delight
Your choice of classic spicy pepperoni or a colorful mix of fresh garden vegetables.

Chinese Cuisine

Starters

Spring Rolls (Veg / Chicken)
Crispy, deep-fried rolls filled with a savory mixture of shredded vegetables or seasoned chicken.
Chilli Paneer / Chilli Chicken
Batter-fried paneer or chicken, tossed in a spicy and tangy sauce with bell peppers and onions.
Hot & Sour Soup
A flavorful soup with mushrooms, tofu, and bamboo shoots, perfectly balanced between spicy and tangy.

Main Course

Hakka Noodles (Veg / Chicken)
Stir-fried noodles tossed with shredded vegetables or chicken in a light soy-sauce seasoning.
Schezwan Fried Rice
Aromatic fried rice wok-tossed with vegetables and a bold, spicy Schezwan sauce.
Kung Pao Chicken
A Sichuan stir-fry with tender chicken, peanuts, and chili peppers in a savory, sweet, and spicy sauce.
